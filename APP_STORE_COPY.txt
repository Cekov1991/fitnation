DESCRIPTION — App Store & Google Play  (4000 char max)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

THIS ISN'T A FITNESS APP. IT'S A MOVEMENT.

Welcome to Fit Nation — the official training platform for gyms and fitness
clubs across North Macedonia. Personalized programs, smart progression, and a
tribe of lifters pulling in the same direction. Pick up the bar. We move
together.

A PROGRAM BUILT AROUND YOU
Tell us your goal, your experience, your schedule, and how long you've got.
Fit Nation builds you a 5-week personalized program — the right split, the
right exercises, the right rep ranges. Want a fresh approach? Regenerate it in
one tap.

LOG LIKE THE NATION DOES
• Live sessions with set-by-set logging (weight, reps, rest)
• Smart prefill from your last performance — no more guessing your starting
  weight
• Built-in rest timer and progression cues
• Personal record alerts the moment you hit them

PROGRESSION THAT ACTUALLY PROGRESSES
Double progression for weighted lifts. Total-reps progression for bodyweight,
TRX, and band work. Fit Nation tells you when you've earned a weight increase —
no more random number-picking, no more stalled programs. The Nation moves
forward.

INSTANT WORKOUT GENERATOR
Don't feel like following the plan today? Open the generator, choose your split
(Push, Pull, Legs, Upper, Lower, or Full Body), filter by available equipment
(barbell, dumbbell, cable, machine, bodyweight, TRX, bands), set your duration,
and Fit Nation builds the session in seconds. Don't like it? Regenerate. Tweak.
Then train.

BUILD YOUR OWN
• Create unlimited custom routines and programs
• Build plans, workouts, and templates from scratch
• Reorder exercises, set targets, swap moves on the fly

YOUR GYM, YOUR NATION
Fit Nation is built for gyms. Every partner gym gets its own branded experience
— logo, colors, and exclusive content — all inside the same app. If your gym is
a Fit Nation partner, browse and clone official programs and routines straight
from your trainer's library. Their programming, your phone, the Nation's
progression engine — fully integrated, fully tracked. Not sure if your gym is
on Fit Nation? You'll see them in the gym selector during sign-up.

TRACK WHAT MATTERS
• Strength score with percentile ranking against lifters at your gym
• Strength balance across 17 muscle groups — the Nation doesn't skip leg day
• Weekly volume, time-under-the-bar, and workout count trends
• Per-exercise charts: max weight, best set reps, total volume — all over time
• Calendar view of every session you've ever logged

THE EXERCISE LIBRARY
Hundreds of exercises with HD videos, muscle-group diagrams, and clear
descriptions. Filter by equipment or muscle group to find exactly what fits your gym today.

WHO TRAINS WITH THE NATION
• Beginners who want a plan that adapts as they grow
• Intermediate lifters tired of guessing weights, rest, and rep targets
• Advanced athletes who want clean data and real progression
• Anyone who's ever tracked workouts in Notes, Excel, or a notebook and knows
  there's a better way

Your goal — fat loss, muscle gain, raw strength, or general fitness — it doesn't
matter. Fit Nation builds the program around you, tracks every rep, and pulls
you forward with the rest of us.

Download Fit Nation. Train with intent. Move with the Nation.
