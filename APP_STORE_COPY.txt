DESCRIPTION — App Store & Google Play  (4000 char max)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOUR GYM. YOUR EQUIPMENT. YOUR TRAINER'S PROGRAM.

Fit Nation is the official training platform of premium gyms across North
Macedonia — built so every workout you do fits the gym you're actually in.

BUILT AROUND YOUR GYM'S EQUIPMENT
Most fitness apps give you generic workouts built for an imaginary gym with
every machine ever made. Fit Nation knows exactly what equipment your gym has.
Every exercise it suggests, every program it generates, every session it builds
— uses only the machines, barbells, cables, and rigs that exist on your gym
floor. You will never be told to do an exercise on equipment your gym doesn't
have.

YOUR TRAINER'S PROGRAMS, IN YOUR POCKET
Your gym's trainers and PTs publish structured programs and routines directly
to you through the app. Browse and clone official programs from your gym's
library — periodized, professional, and built for your specific equipment.
Track your trainer's programming rep by rep, automatically progressed over
time.

A PROGRAM BUILT AROUND YOU
Tell us your goal, your experience, your schedule, and how long you've got.
Fit Nation builds you a personalized 5-week program — the right split, the
right exercises for your gym, the right rep ranges. Want a fresh approach?
Regenerate it in one tap.

LOG LIKE THE NATION DOES
• Live sessions with set-by-set logging (weight, reps, rest)
• Smart prefill from your last performance — no more guessing your starting
  weight
• Built-in rest timer and progression cues
• Personal record alerts the moment you hit them

PROGRESSION THAT ACTUALLY PROGRESSES
Double progression for weighted lifts. Total-reps progression for bodyweight,
TRX, and band work. Fit Nation tells you when you've earned a weight increase —
no more random number-picking, no more stalled programs.

INSTANT WORKOUT GENERATOR
Don't feel like following the plan today? Open the generator, choose your focus
(Push, Pull, Legs, Upper, Lower, or Full Body), set your duration, and Fit
Nation builds a session in seconds — using only the equipment your gym has.
Don't like it? Regenerate. Tweak. Then train.

BUILD YOUR OWN
• Create unlimited custom routines and programs
• Build plans, workouts, and templates from scratch
• Reorder exercises, set targets, swap moves on the fly

TRACK WHAT MATTERS
• Strength score with percentile ranking against members at your gym
• Strength balance across 17 muscle groups
• Weekly volume, time-under-the-bar, and workout count trends
• Per-exercise charts: max weight, best set reps, total volume — all over time
• Calendar view of every session you've ever logged

THE EXERCISE LIBRARY
Every exercise is tagged to the equipment it requires and filtered to what your
gym has on the floor. HD videos, muscle-group diagrams, and clear descriptions
— no phantom machines, no wasted browsing.

WHO TRAINS WITH THE NATION
• Members of premium gyms in North Macedonia who want a real structured plan
• Anyone tired of apps that recommend equipment their gym doesn't have
• Lifters who want their trainer's programming tracked and progressed
  automatically
• Beginners who want a plan that starts with what's available to them today
• Advanced athletes who want clean data and real, earned progression

Your goal — fat loss, muscle gain, raw strength, or general fitness — it doesn't
matter. Fit Nation builds the program around you and your gym, tracks every rep,
and pulls you forward with the rest of us.

Download Fit Nation. Train in your gym. Move with the Nation.
